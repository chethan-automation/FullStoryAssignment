Test Plan

1) Verify FS reporting of adding an item to the cart and checking it out - @automated
2) Verify click event is captured in the FS event bundle after the operation
3) Verify page navigation is captured in the FS bundle (ex: navigating from homepage to cart page)
4) Verify blocked elements are not captured by FS (ex: credit card info)
5) Verify page API response include configured blocked elements
6) Verify heartbeat events are sent even in user inactivity state- @automated
7) Verify that XHR network requests made by the application are recorded (ex: bundle requests)
8) Verify that errors are captured by bundle events (Ex: clicking on purchase without customer details for shipping and payment)
